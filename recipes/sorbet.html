Mango Sorbet

Description

This dreamy mango sorbet is a deliciously refreshing dessert to make with fresh ripe mangos to cool off on hot summer days. Hope you enjoy this as much as my family does!

From Allrecipes, uploaded by Cindy Chaney

Ingredients

  • 4 mangos - peeled, seeded, and cubed
  • 1 cup simple syrup or to taste
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice

Steps

  1. Place cubed mango into a food processor; blend until smooth.
  2. Pour in simple syrup and lime juice to taste; process until smooth.
  3. Transfer mango mixture into an ice cream maker and freeze according to manufacturer's instructions, about 20 minutes.
